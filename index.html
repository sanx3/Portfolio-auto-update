About Me

Shaping the
Final Sound

I am Sanjith, a Re-Recording Mix Engineer based in Kerala, specialising in Malayalam cinema's post-production sound. Over 3+ years in the industry, I have crafted the final sonic identity of 40+ feature films, blending dialogue, music, and effects into seamless cinematic experiences.

My work spans major commercial releases with Kerala's biggest stars — from Dileep and Mohanlal to Anoop Menon and Dhyan Sreenivasan. I bring precision, creativity, and technical excellence to every final mix, ensuring each film sounds exactly as the director envisions it.

With deep expertise in theatrical mixing, OTT deliveries, and immersive audio formats, I am committed to pushing the sonic boundaries of Malayalam cinema forward.

What I Do

Areas of Expertise

01

Re-Recording Mix

Crafting the final audio mix for theatrical and OTT releases — balancing dialogue, music, and effects to deliver the director's vision.

02

Dolby Atmos, 5.1 & Stereo

Immersive audio mixing for premium theatrical releases and streaming platforms, creating 3D soundscapes that surround the audience. Also delivering crisp stereo mixes for broadcast and digital platforms.

03

Music Integration

Seamlessly blending BGM and songs into the final mix, working closely with composers to achieve the perfect emotional balance.

04

Dialogue Clarity

Ensuring crystal-clear intelligibility of performances, with meticulous editing and dynamic processing for every scene.

05

OTT Mastering

Platform-specific loudness compliance and delivery specs for Amazon Prime, Netflix, Disney+ Hotstar, and SonyLIV.

Sound Effects

Curating and designing the complete SFX layer — from ambient textures to cinematic impacts — that grounds every scene in reality.
